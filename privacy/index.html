Privacy, without fog.

This pilot currently collects less data because that is the sensible default.

What this site stores

The site stores one preference on your device if you use the light/dark theme control. That preference is not used to identify you or follow you around the web.

Analytics and cookies

There are no analytics, advertising pixels or non-essential cookies in this pilot. Accordingly, there is no consent banner. If that changes, this notice and the consent experience must change before the tracking is enabled.

Forms

The visible feedback forms are not connected to a processor yet, so this build does not transmit or retain their contents. Before collection is enabled, the form will name the processor, purpose, retention period and contact route.

External links

Links to venues and ticket pages take you to other organisations. Their privacy practices apply once you leave this site.

Contact

This is a private validation pilot and is not yet collecting personal data. A dedicated privacy contact will be added before any public form is switched on.
