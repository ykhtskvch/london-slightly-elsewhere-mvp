Hampstead When You Want to Pretend You Left London

Old lanes, heath air and a pub finish with enough weather to make the story better.

Start
Hampstead
Time
3–4 hours
Walk
Medium
Budget
Under £30
Easy exit
Possible
old lanesheath edgequiet streetpub

The useful version

Let the village streets arrive before the heath. The walk gives a date room to breathe; the pub gives it an ending. Take the shorter lanes-and-pub version if the weather turns.

What not to expect

Not flat, not cheap-cheap, and not for people who want every stop managed in advance.
