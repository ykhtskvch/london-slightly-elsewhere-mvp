Bloomsbury After Rain

Squares, books and somewhere warm: an indoor-outdoor route for days that should not be written off.

Start
Russell Square or Tottenham Court Road
Time
2–4 hours
Walk
Very low
Budget
Free-ish to £30
Easy exit
Built in
squarebookshopindoor stopwarm finish

The useful version

Move between short outdoor sections and warm interiors: square, bookshop, one cultural stop, then an old room. The weather is part of the atmosphere rather than a scheduling problem.

What not to expect

Not a grand museum day or a restaurant crawl. It is deliberately small-scale.
