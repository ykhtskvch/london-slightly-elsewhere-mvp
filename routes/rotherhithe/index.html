Rotherhithe for River Air and Old Dock Melancholy

Warehouses, water and an old-room finish for a quieter stretch of the Thames.

Start
Canada Water or Rotherhithe
Time
3–4 hours
Walk
Gentle
Budget
Under £30
Easy exit
Possible
river startdock walkwide viewpub

The useful version

Follow the older dock edge, pause where the river opens out and finish in a verified old room.

What not to expect

More atmospheric than eventful; several stretches are deliberately quiet.
