Putney for River Air and a Proper Music Pub

A Thames evening of river air, a proper pub, dinner and optional live music if the mood holds.

The useful version

Walk by the Thames, pause at The Duke’s Head, have dinner at Al Forno and then decide whether the evening should remain a conversation or become a gig at The Half Moon. The no-gig route is the route, not a consolation prize.

What not to expect

Personally checked, but live opening hours, dinner availability and gig listings can change. Check them before setting off.
