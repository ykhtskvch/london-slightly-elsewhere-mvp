The useful version

Walk by the Thames, pause in a currently verified pub, then decide whether the evening should remain a conversation or become a gig. The no-gig route is the route, not a consolation prize.

What not to expect

Atmospheric rather than glamorous. Check current opening hours, listings and tickets before setting off.
